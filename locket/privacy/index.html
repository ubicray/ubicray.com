Privacy Policy

Locket

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Locket is designed to keep your journal content on your device.

What Locket stores

Journal entries, transcribed text, moods, cues, chat history, settings, and related local model data are stored on your device.

What Locket sends

Locket does not send your thoughts or spoken input to servers for AI processing or training. Speech-to-text runs on-device, audio is not stored.

Network use

On first launch, Locket downloads public AI model files from Hugging Face into app-private storage. After download, inference runs locally on-device.

Your control

You can remove app data by deleting journal entries inside the app or uninstalling the app from your device.

Questions

For privacy questions, please use the developer contact listed on the app's Google Play listing.
